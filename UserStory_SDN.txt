Audience: People who want to understand the poetic beauty of Latin, yet have not
had a bunch of training in Latin. This can be students.
Purpose: The audience wants to not only read the Latin, but also understand the
literary devices that are at work, and how such literary devices convey the
story in a more beautiful and poetic manner.
Needs: We need the rules for Latin dactylic hexameters.
Skill level: None
